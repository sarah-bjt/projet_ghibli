News

Miyazaki's Retirement

Hayao Miyazaki, one of the founders of Studio Ghibli, has announced his retirement multiple times but has returned to create new films. His dedication to his craft and storytelling has kept him active in the industry.

Ghibli Park Opening Delay

Studio Ghibli's theme park, initially scheduled to open in 2022 in Aichi Prefecture, Japan, was delayed due to the impact of the COVID-19 pandemic. The park, designed to feature attractions inspired by Ghibli films, was highly anticipated by fans worldwide.

Ghibli's First 3D CG Film

Studio Ghibli was reported to be working on its first 3D computer-generated (CG) animated film. Directed by Ghibli co-founder Hayao Miyazaki, the film was said to be titled "How Do You Live?" and was based on the 1937 novel of the same name.

Ghibli's Move to Digital Animation

There were reports about Studio Ghibli transitioning to digital animation for future projects. Traditionally known for its hand-drawn animation, this move marked a significant shift in the studio's approach to filmmaking.
